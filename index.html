Reasons to be a Hero

Be Happier

About 76% of people who use a mindfulness & meditation practice say that general wellness is their primary motive. Meditation has been shown to improve anxiety levels 60% of the time.

Improve Memory & Focus

Practicing meditation can increase your attention span after only 4 days. Meditation can also increase grey matter which also plays an important role in memory and learning.

Sleep Better

Get a good nights sleep and help turn your brain off after a long day. Meditation has been shown to reduce the wake time of people with insomnia by 50%.
